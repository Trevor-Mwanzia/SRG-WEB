About STEM Resource Group

Founded in 2019 by Kate Mwanzia-Kagema, SRG is dedicated to spreading hope, creating awareness, and fostering independence for children with neurological conditions.

Our Story

STEM Resource Group began as a vision to create a more inclusive world for neurodiverse children. Through collaboration with advocates like Sandy in South Africa, we've built an international network of support and hope.

Compassionate Care

Every child deserves understanding and support

Our Journey

Launch of donation drives and community outreach programs across Nairobi

Collaboration with Advocate Mother Sandy and expansion to Morocco, Tunisia partnerships
